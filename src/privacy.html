Nudge — Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 1, 2026

What Nudge accesses

Nudge requests read-only access to your Google Calendar, Google Tasks, and Google Contacts. This is used solely to display your meetings, tasks, and attendee photos on your device.

What we store

Nothing leaves your device. Nudge runs entirely in your browser. There is no server, no database, and no analytics. Your Google authentication token is stored in your browser's local storage so you don't have to sign in every time.

What we share

Nothing. Your data is never sent to any third party, collected, or tracked.

Contact

If you have questions, open an issue at github.com/HowdyMoto/Calendar.
